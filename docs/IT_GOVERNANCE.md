# IT Governance Framework

Comprehensive IT governance framework for Federated Group.

## Purpose

Establish effective IT governance to ensure IT investments align with business objectives, manage risks, and deliver value.

## Governance Structure

### IT Steering Committee

**Purpose**: Strategic IT decision-making

**Members**:
- IT Director (Chair)
- CEO/President
- CFO
- Business Unit Leaders
- Key Stakeholders

**Responsibilities**:
- Approve IT strategy and roadmap
- Review major IT investments
- Oversee IT risk management
- Ensure IT-business alignment

**Meeting Frequency**: Quarterly

### IT Operations Committee

**Purpose**: Operational IT management

**Members**:
- IT Director
- IT Team Leads
- Business Process Owners
- Security Representative

**Responsibilities**:
- Review operational performance
- Address operational issues
- Approve operational changes
- Monitor service levels

**Meeting Frequency**: Monthly

### Change Advisory Board (CAB)

**Purpose**: Change management oversight

**Members**:
- IT Director
- Technical Lead
- Business Representative
- Security Representative

**Responsibilities**:
- Review and approve changes
- Assess change risks
- Schedule changes
- Monitor change success

**Meeting Frequency**: Weekly or as needed

## Governance Processes

### Strategic Planning

#### IT Strategy Development

1. **Business Alignment**
   - Understand business objectives
   - Identify IT requirements
   - Align IT strategy with business

2. **Technology Assessment**
   - Evaluate current technology
   - Assess technology trends
   - Identify opportunities

3. **Strategy Documentation**
   - Document IT strategy
   - Create technology roadmap
   - Define success metrics

4. **Approval Process**
   - Present to IT Steering Committee
   - Obtain approval
   - Communicate strategy

#### Annual Planning

- **Q4**: Develop next year's plan
- **Q1**: Finalize and approve plan
- **Quarterly**: Review and adjust
- **Annually**: Comprehensive review

### Investment Management

#### IT Investment Process

1. **Business Case Development**
   - Define business need
   - Evaluate options
   - Calculate ROI
   - Assess risks

2. **Investment Review**
   - IT Operations Committee review
   - Technical assessment
   - Financial analysis
   - Risk assessment

3. **Approval**
   - IT Steering Committee approval
   - Budget allocation
   - Project initiation

4. **Monitoring**
   - Track progress
   - Monitor benefits
   - Report status
   - Adjust as needed

#### Investment Categories

- **Strategic**: Long-term competitive advantage
- **Tactical**: Operational improvements
- **Maintenance**: Keep systems current
- **Compliance**: Regulatory requirements

### Risk Management

#### IT Risk Assessment

1. **Risk Identification**
   - Technology risks
   - Operational risks
   - Security risks
   - Compliance risks

2. **Risk Analysis**
   - Assess probability
   - Assess impact
   - Calculate risk score
   - Prioritize risks

3. **Risk Mitigation**
   - Develop mitigation strategies
   - Implement controls
   - Monitor effectiveness
   - Update as needed

4. **Risk Reporting**
   - Regular risk reports
   - Executive updates
   - Board reporting (if applicable)

### Performance Management

#### IT Performance Metrics

**Strategic Metrics**:
- IT-business alignment score
- Strategic initiative progress
- Innovation index
- Customer satisfaction

**Operational Metrics**:
- System uptime
- Incident resolution time
- Service level achievement
- Cost per transaction

**Financial Metrics**:
- IT budget variance
- Cost per user
- ROI on investments
- Total cost of ownership

#### Performance Reporting

- **Monthly**: Operational metrics
- **Quarterly**: Strategic metrics
- **Annually**: Comprehensive review

### Compliance and Audit

#### Compliance Management

1. **Regulatory Requirements**
   - Identify applicable regulations
   - Assess compliance status
   - Implement controls
   - Monitor compliance

2. **Internal Policies**
   - Develop IT policies
   - Communicate policies
   - Enforce policies
   - Review and update

3. **Audit Management**
   - Prepare for audits
   - Respond to findings
   - Implement remediation
   - Track progress

## Decision-Making Framework

### Decision Authority Matrix

| Decision Type | Authority | Approval Required |
|--------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Strategic IT Initiatives | IT Steering Committee | CEO |
| Major Investments (>$100K) | IT Steering Committee | CFO, CEO |
| Operational Changes | IT Operations Committee | IT Director |
| Standard Changes | Pre-approved | None |
| Emergency Changes | IT Director | Post-approval |

### Decision Criteria

1. **Business Value**: Alignment with business objectives
2. **Technical Feasibility**: Technical capability and resources
3. **Financial Impact**: Cost and ROI
4. **Risk Assessment**: Risk level and mitigation
5. **Resource Availability**: People, time, budget
