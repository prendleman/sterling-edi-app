# Technology Roadmap

Strategic technology roadmap for the EDI application and related systems.

## Vision

Build a modern, scalable, and integrated EDI platform that supports business growth and operational excellence.

## Current State (2025)

### Technology Stack
- **EDI Platform**: IBM Sterling B2B Integrator
- **ERP**: Acumatica
- **CRM**: Acumatica (built-in)
- **eCommerce**: Shopify/Magento/WooCommerce
- **Database**: SQL Server
- **Analytics**: Power BI
- **Infrastructure**: On-premise/Cloud hybrid

### Capabilities
- EDI processing (X12, EDIFACT)
- ERP/CRM integration
- eCommerce integration
- Basic AI/automation
- Reporting and dashboards

## Risk Management

### Technology Risks

1. **Vendor Lock-in**: Dependence on specific vendors
2. **Technology Obsolescence**: Outdated technologies
3. **Integration Complexity**: Complex integrations
4. **Skills Gap**: Lack of expertise

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Multi-Vendor Strategy**: Avoid single vendor dependence
2. **Continuous Evaluation**: Regular technology assessment
3. **Standardization**: Use standard technologies
4. **Training**: Invest in team development
